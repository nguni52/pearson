Comparing global development

People, natural resources and wealth are not evenly distributed across the world. In economic and social terms, the world is generally divided into a Developed North and a Developing South. People in developed countries (MEDCs) generally enjoy a higher standard of living, a longer life expectancy and more food security than those living in developing countries (LEDCs). Many MEDCs donate food, technical expertise and funds to LEDCs in order to encourage and assist social and economic development in these countries.
