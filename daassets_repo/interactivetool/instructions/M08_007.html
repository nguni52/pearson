Hemispheres

Places and features on the globe can be placed into two hemispheres (northern or southern hemisphere) and (eastern or western hemisphere) or four quadrants (NW, NE, SW, SE).

The equator divides the Earth into the northern and southern hemisphere. The Greenwich meridian and the 180° line of longitude divides the Earth into the western and eastern hemisphere.

Hemispheres are important when it comes to giving the co-ordinates of places.
