Ratio scales

A ratio scale compares distances on the map to distances on the ground in the same measurement.


A ratio scale is always given in the format of 1 : x.
1 unit on the map : x units on the ground.

Ratios can also be written as fractions, i.e. 1 x , and that is why ratio scales are also called representative fractions (RF).
