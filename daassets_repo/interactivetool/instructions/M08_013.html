Satellite images

Satellite images are made from a collection of data obtained by different sensors on satellites. These sensors send back information to receivers on Earth. Computers then process this information for a wide range of different uses.

Satellite images may show more than what our eyes can see. Human eyes can only view visible light. Sensors on satellites can pick up other frequencies of the electromagnetic spectrum.
