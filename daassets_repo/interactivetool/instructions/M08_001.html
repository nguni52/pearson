Latitude and longitude, degrees and minutes

Co-ordinates of places are used to give location. We express the co-ordinates in degrees (°), minutes (') and seconds (").

We read the latitude reference on the side margins of the map and the longitude reference on the top or bottom of the map.

As we zoom in and the scale of the map gets bigger, so we need to give more information for locating places on the map. Finding location of places on the political map of Africa requires only degrees. For a provincial map you will need to give degrees and minutes, and for a topographic map at a scale of 1 : 50 000, you will need to give degrees, minutes and seconds. This will be illustrated to you when you do the activities.
