Interpreting climatic information from different images

South Africa Climate

Wind roses help us to visualise predominant wind directions. They are circular images that show the frequency of wind speed and wind direction at a particular place over a certain period of time. Each spoke on the wind rose represents a specific direction that the wind blows from.
