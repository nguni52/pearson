Sustainability: Conserving natural resources

The ultimate benefit of sound environmental management of our natural resources is a better quality of life for everyone on the planet. Nations, businesses and communities should have an environmental conservation policy if they wish to be sustainable. Conserving biomes and our natural resources should be a priority for all individuals and communities. Climate change is resulting in biomes becoming threatened and certain plant and animal species facing extinction.

How can South Africa achieve a sustainable use of resources?

  • Good governance
  • Policing (Green Scorpions)
  • Policy (conserving and protecting biomes)
  • International treaties (Ramsar Convention)
  • Mapping resources (GIS technology)
  • Education and public awareness
  • Changing attitudes and values
  • Ecotourism
  • 3 Rs (Reduce, Recycle, Reuse)
