Measuring distance using a line and ratio scale

The ratio scale on the 1 : 50 000 topographic map compares one unit on the map to 50 000 of the same unit on the ground. This means that 1 cm : 50 000 cm. In reality we use kilometres and since there are 100 000 cm in 1 km, we can say that 1 cm : 0,5 km.
