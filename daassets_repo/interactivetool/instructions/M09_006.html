Reading height off maps and orthophoto maps

Height on orthophoto maps is shown in four ways:

  • Contour lines (5 m contour interval)
  • Trig. beacons
  • Benchmarks
  • Spot heights

    • Orthophoto maps (B) have the benefit of being an aerial image (A) with the advantages of having many of the symbols on a topographic map printed on it (C).
